About Me

I am a full stack developer originally from Rochester, New York. I graduated from Rochester Institute of Technology in 2018 with a bachelor's degree in chemistry and a focus on inorganic chemistry and mathematics.

My interest in web development and programming in general comes from my experience while doing research at RIT. I was tasked with imrpoving the programs that were used to run our instrumentation, as well as developing new programs. Since this was my first foray into programming, I had to learn on the fly by looking into other programming languages like javascript.

Since then, I have become fully devoted to becoming a full time web developer. I am currently enrolled in the Georgia Tech web development bootcamp, and I am scheduled to graduate in late May 2019.
